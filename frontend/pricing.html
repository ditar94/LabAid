Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?
Sign up and your lab is ready instantly. You get full access to all Standard features for 7 days. No credit card required to start.
Is there a per-user fee?
No. The Standard plan includes unlimited users for your lab. Everyone from lab directors to technicians can access the system at no extra cost.
Can I pay monthly instead of annually?
We currently only offer annual billing. This keeps things simple and gives you the best value for your lab.
What happens after my trial ends?
We'll send you a payment link before your trial expires. If you don't subscribe, your lab will be read-only — no data is ever deleted. You can subscribe at any time to pick up right where you left off.
What if I want to cancel?
The Standard plan is billed annually. At the end of your year, you can simply choose not to renew. Your data is never deleted — you'll always have read-only access to everything in your lab.
