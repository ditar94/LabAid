Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?
Email us and we'll create your lab within one business day. You get full access to all Standard features for 14 days. No credit card required to start.
Is there a per-user fee?
No. The Standard plan includes unlimited users for your lab. Everyone from lab directors to technicians can access the system at no extra cost.
Can I pay monthly instead of annually?
We currently only offer annual billing. This keeps things simple and gives you the best value for your lab.
What happens after my trial ends?
We'll send you a payment link before your trial expires. If you don't subscribe, your lab will be read-only — no data is ever deleted.
Do you offer discounts for academic labs?
Yes — contact us to discuss academic pricing for university and research institution labs.
